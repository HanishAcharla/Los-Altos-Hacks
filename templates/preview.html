What Happens Next?

Our AI model will analyze your leaf image using a specialized ResNet-50 deep learning model trained on thousands of apple leaf images. The system can detect several common apple leaf diseases:

  • Frog Eye Leaf Spot
  • Powdery Mildew
  • Apple Rust
  • Apple Scab
  • Complex (multiple conditions)
  • Healthy leaves

After analysis, you'll receive a detailed report with disease information and management recommendations.
